An \char`\"{}\+Orphan Entity\char`\"{} is an entity lacking a parent entity, such as an \char`\"{}\+H\+B\+A Port\char`\"{} without a \char`\"{}host\char`\"{} parent, or a \char`\"{}iomodule\char`\"{} without a parent \char`\"{}storagearray\char`\"{} entity.
